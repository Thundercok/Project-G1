# Project G1 — Story Bible

An original story in the spirit of 1998: an ordinary senior engineer, a catastrophic
experiment failure, a government that sends soldiers to execute all witnesses, and a
stranger in a suit who is always, always watching.

## Core Premise & Status

```
STATUS: EXPERIMENT FAILED
FACILITY STATUS: OVERRIDDEN BY ALIEN HORDE
GOVERNMENT ORDERS: WITNESS TERMINATION IN EFFECT (RESIDUE CLEANUP)
DIRECTIVE: ESCAPE NOW!
```

## Setting

**The Corvus Deep Research Annex** — a subterranean materials-science facility
built into a decommissioned missile silo complex. Sub-Level C hosted **Project G1**: an attempt
to hold open a microscopic aperture called the **Threshold**.

## Characters

- **Chad Thundercock** (the player) — a senior test engineer in an orange Class-IV
  hazard suit with a Λ calibration emblem. Wrong seniority to refuse the
  morning's test. Right suit and gun skills to survive it.
- **The Auditor** (the villain model) — a gaunt man in a dark suit with a
  briefcase who appears in places no one could reach, observes, and is gone
  when you look twice.
- **The Sweepers** (HECU-style soldiers) — a military containment unit whose
  orders are not evacuation. Their order is witness execution.
- **The Taken** (zombies) — Annex staff, changed by what came through.
- **The Strays** (aliens) — aggressive fauna from the far side of the Threshold.

## The Threshold Event

During a routine widening test, the Threshold suffered a catastrophic resonance cascade —
**EXPERIMENT FAILED**. The aperture blew wide open, allowing alien hordes to overrun the facility.
Topside, the government activated containment protocol: execute all witnesses and leave no residue.

## Chapters (one per level)

### Chapter One — COLD START (Level 1, `TestScene`)
Chad Thundercock survives the event in Sub-Level C.
Escape through the lab corridor, control room, and industrial hall — past zombies, alien specimens, and government sweepers who open fire on sight.
*The Auditor watches from behind the Control Room glass.*

### Chapter Two — QUARANTINE (Level 2, `Level2`)
The elevator opens on the surface motor pool at dawn.
Military sweepers and helicopters hold the surface yard, executing any survivors.
Chad Thundercock goes back down into the maintenance shaft to escape through the ancient Undercroft.
*The Auditor stands on the perimeter wall above the patrols.*

### Chapter Three — THRESHOLD (Level 3, `Level3`)
The Undercroft: grown over with pods and spore light.
The Threshold ring hangs at its center — aliens pour through.
Chad Thundercock fights through the final guardians and steps into the portal.
